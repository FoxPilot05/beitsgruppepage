ABOUT BEITSGRUPPE OST

Shaping the future of the IT industry

Beitsgruppe Ost is a leading provider of hardware and software solutions, specializing in tailor-made IT infrastructures for companies of all sizes. Our customer structure includes small businesses (SMEs), medium-sized companies, and large enterprises. We offer customized solutions and comprehensive support options to meet the different needs of our customers.

CAREER AT BEITSGRUPPE OST

Join us in shaping the digital future

Software Developer

Responsibility for the development and implementation of customized software solutions for our customers.

Requirements: Experience in software development, knowledge of common programming languages, ability to work in a team.

IT Service Technician

Installation, maintenance, and support of our customers' IT infrastructure.

Requirements: Technical know-how, service orientation, experience in support.

Sales Representative

Advising and selling our IT products and services to new and existing customers.

Requirements: Experience in sales, strong communication skills, enjoy customer contact.
