About Beitsgruppe Ost

Beitsgruppe Ost is a leading provider of hardware and software solutions, specializing in tailor-made IT infrastructures for companies of all sizes.

Our customer structure includes small businesses (SMEs), medium-sized companies and large enterprises. We offer customized solutions and comprehensive support options to meet the different needs of our customers.
