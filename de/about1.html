Who we are:

Beitsgruppe Ost - your partner for hardware and software solutions

Kennt ihr das, wenn Ihr eine Idee für ein Start-Up habt, jedoch technisch unerfahren seid und euch nun fragt, wie Ihr das umsetzen könnt? Dieses Problem haben viele neue Start-Ups und daher haben wir, die Beitsgruppe Ost, uns zusammengetan, um euch zu unterstützen.

We understand that the path from idea to implementation involves many technical challenges. That's why we offer customized solutions that make your dreams come true. Our strength lies in putting together an optimized and cost-effective selection of hardware and integrating the right software so that you can get started quickly and efficiently.

A concrete example: Smart City Solutions

One example of our work is the start-up Smart City Solutions. We provided them with an extensive range of hardware and software so that they could implement their idea for the digital administration of municipalities.

We also supported the Smart City Solutions team by programming the first prototype of their software, which they needed to turn their vision into reality. Thanks to our support, they were able to grow faster and realize their ideas more efficiently.

Our Services

What we can do for you

We offer start-ups and small to medium-sized companies comprehensive support in the following areas:

  • Hardware procurement: We put together the right hardware required for your projects and take care of procurement and integration.
  • Software-Solutions: We develop customized software solutions or offer you tried-and-tested standard software that is perfectly tailored to your requirements.
  • Prototyping: We help you to develop the first prototype of your software or hardware and ensure that your idea can be tested and implemented quickly.
