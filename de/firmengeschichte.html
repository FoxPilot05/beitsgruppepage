Who we are

We are a mixed team of people interested in technology who have come together from different backgrounds to found our wonderful company, the “Beitsgruppe Ost”.

Our backgrounds include special activities, some job titles such as: “Data scientist of the Manhattan Project”, “Actual Microsoft founder”, “Inventor of the pixel” and “Skynet Application Developer”.
